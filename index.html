Sri Lanka Medical Council

Temporary Registration — Section 67A | Category C  ·  Approved 25.01.2019

Before you begin

  1. Temporary Registration is granted for a maximum of 12 months, restricted to the specified institution.
  2. All documents must be originals or certified copies. Certificate of Good Standing must be issued within the last 6 months.
  3. Registration fee: Rs 38,500.00 per applicant. Attach proof of payment.
  4. Submit renewal applications at least 4 weeks before expiry through the same authorities.
